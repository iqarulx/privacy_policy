Location

Required

We need location permission to:

  • Verify you are within the designated geo-fencing area when punching in/out
  • Record latitude and longitude coordinates for attendance verification
  • Prevent time tracking from unauthorized locations
  • Ensure compliance with workplace location policies

Your Privacy Matters

We respect your privacy and only use these permissions for the specific purposes mentioned above. We do not access your messages, location, or other data unless it's directly related to payroll and attendance functions.

We never:

  • Read your personal messages unrelated to attendance
  • Track your location outside of work hours
  • Share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Store more information than necessary for payroll functions
