Privacy Policy for ConnectStaff

Last updated: June 04, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information based on your permissions and app usage:

  • Location Data: Used to track employee location for attendance and field tracking purposes.
  • Call Log Data: Accessed only if required by an organization for communication audit purposes and only with proper consent.
  • Network and Internet Information: Used to detect internet availability and optimize app performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To enable attendance and tracking features based on your location.
  • To support background services (e.g., location tracking when the app is not active).
  • To restart services automatically after device reboot (using boot completed permission).
  • To optionally log call data if your employer configures it as part of your work role.

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

3. User Consent

  • We display clear in-app consent dialogs before accessing any sensitive data.
  • You may decline permissions, though some features may be limited.
  • Call log access is only used after explicit opt-in and if required by your organization.

4. Permissions Requested

Below is a list of permissions we request, along with the purpose:

  • INTERNET – To communicate with backend servers.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE – To check network connectivity.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION, ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION – For real-time and background location tracking features.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED – To restart location services after device reboot.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_LOCATION – For continuous tracking while the app runs in the background.
  • READ_CALL_LOG – Optional, used only when needed for call log auditing in enterprise settings.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as it is required for operational or reporting purposes and in compliance with your organization's data policy.

6. Security

We implement strong security measures to safeguard your data, including encryption, secure data transfer, and access control.

7. Children’s Privacy

ConnectStaff is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date above or via in-app notification.
